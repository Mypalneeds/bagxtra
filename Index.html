Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know my item will actually arrive?
Your payment stays in escrow until you confirm delivery. Travelers are verified and must provide photo proof and a unique code at handoff. If something goes wrong, you get refunded.
Do I have to pay upfront for the item I'm delivering?
Yes, but shoppers pay first and funds are locked in escrow. Once the item is delivered, your payout (including commission) is released instantly.
How do I partner with BagXtra as a vendor?
Vendors can list their store or become an official drop-off/pickup partner. We drive traffic to your business and pay you per handoff. Just join the waitlist and we'll reach out.
Is this legal and safe in my country?
Yes. We only allow store-bought goods from verified retailers. No personal parcels or unknown items. Travelers are ID verified and we provide customs support.
What happens if the traveler cancels or my item is delayed?
We'll rematch your delivery with the next available verified traveler. If nothing works in your timeframe, we'll refund you or let you wait for a new offer.
