Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Bagxtra

How does the AI matching system work?
Our AI analyzes 50+ factors including travel routes, timing, capacity, user ratings, item compatibility, and behavioral patterns to match shoppers with the most suitable travelers, ensuring 95% successful deliveries.
What happens if my item gets lost or damaged?
All items are covered by comprehensive insurance up to $5,000. In the rare event of loss or damage, we provide full compensation and work with local authorities to recover items when possible.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, biometric verification, and AI-powered fraud detection. Your data is never shared and all transactions are protected by escrow.
What countries will you support?
We're launching with major routes between USA, Canada, UK, Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and expanding to 50+ countries by end of 2026.
